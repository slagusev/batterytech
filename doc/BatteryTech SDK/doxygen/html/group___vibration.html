Vibration Control

Support for vibration effect playback. More...

Detailed Description

Most systems do not have robust vibration control, but specifically on Android, there are systems such as Immersion Technology's Motiv or UHL which can play a variety of effects at various intensities. Such systems will need to be integrated on the Android platform side. Stock Android vibration support can also be integrated instead. Please follow the instructions in the section on Integrating platform-specific systems for additional information.
